TABIKA KEMAS
Tabika Kemas I started my studies in a neat kindergarten at the age of 5 to 6 years old. This kindergarten is located close to my house which only takes 10 minutes to reach. At first, I was a bit timid to meet or meet new friends but after almost 2 years there I had many friends who later went to the same primary school as me. Not many memories I can remember during kindergarten, but what i remember is that the food prepared there is very tasty and a lot of toys taht I can play. After 2 years I finished my studies here and continued my studies to the primary school level.
SK SERI BAYU
Next, I continued my studies to Sekolah Kebangsaan Seri Bayu, Penang from 2007 to 2012. At first, I was so excited to enter this school I was looking forward to what I would face, but as a relatively quiet person I also felt a little scared. Eventhough I am like that, I was able to get through it successfully. In this school I am required to participate in 3 co-curricular activities. Namely the uniformed body of the School Youth Cadet, visual arts club and netball sports club. But I am not a very active person in co-curricular activities. Despite that thank you to the art club, due to the activities of this club I was able to find out my interest more in the field of art. Besides that, in my studies I got some awards but I am not a very smart person, I am just a normal student =D. After 6 years of being there, I finished primary school with a 4A 1B result in the UPSR.
SMK TELOK KUMBAR
After that, I continued my studies to the secondary school level from 2013 to 2017. At first, I wanted to register as an MRSM student but I did not pass their qualifications. So both my parents sent me to a regular secondary school which is SMK Telok KumbarSMKTK. Here I am a hostel student, although it is only a rural school it has a daily dormitory that can accommodate a small number of students not like a full dormitory. Life in hostel is an unforgettable memory. There I began to learn to be independent and know a lot of types of people. After three years of schooling, in 2015 I sat for the PT3 exam and I got 4A out of 9 subjects. After that, I was taken into a pure science stream class. This flow class is really challenging for me who is less proficient in math. Therefore, to cover the weakness I took the art subject in addition as my teacher's recommendation to sit for SPM. So, the total number of subjects I took was 10 subjects. For your information, I am not a biology student but I am an Engineering Drawing student. This subject is a subject that I am quite interested in. After 2 years of hard work, I sat for SPM and obtained 4A out of ten subjects taken. I feel so grateful for this meager success, yet it is enough for me who is relatively weak in the subject of pure science class.
